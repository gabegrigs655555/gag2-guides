Inferno Ritual

The Campfire Keeper is here. Speak to him to learn how to perform an inferno ritual to power up your pet with a new mutation. The ritual requires a designated pet and Inferno Shards earned from submitting fruits to Bearnaby.

Activate the ritual by reaching tier 1 in the campfire. The higher the campfire tier, the faster the ritual is performed. Once the ritual is complete, your pet receives the Inferno mutation.

The ritual consumes the Inferno Shards you place in, and any existing pet mutations on the selected pet are replaced with Inferno once complete.
