Bearnaby

Bearnaby would like your help. He is hungry and would like you to bring him some foraged crops fom your garden to cleanse his appetite. Bearnaby will be looking for crops with a specific mutation, trait, and weight. Handing in the crop he is looking for will earn you an exclusive campfire seed pack, and every 50 submissions an exotic version.

Bearnaby will also award the player an Inferno Shard for every 25 plant submissions.

Plant mutations and weights will change as you progress through submissions. Requested crop traits will alternate every 30 minutes.
