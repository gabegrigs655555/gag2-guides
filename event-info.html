Event Info

Get your camping gear ready as the 2026 Campfire Event is here! Warm temperatures and Cozy nights, gather around the Summer Campfire and prepare to roast some of your garden harvest. With all new seeds, pets, and cosmetics to unlock; you are bound to have a wonderful camping trip.

But it wont be camp if you're just given the rewards. No no no... get your scout sashes on and give it some good 'ole DIY (Do It Yourself) Energy. During this event, rewards are earnt through crafting.

How do we begin? Lets ask Camper Carl, our visiting Summer Campfire Expert!

Part Two

The campfire is heating up, and things are getting toasty. There wont be a summer campfire without some company!

A hungry grizzly visitor has arrived for Summer Campfire event part two. Bearnaby is here to take some of you Scouts campfire foraging. Talk to him to begin and recieve some exclusive new campfire seeds. Maybe even a chance at some sought after rainbow variants!

Part Three

The campfire roars on! Part three of the Summer Campfire event brings a new mysterious guest and some new added DIY recipes to the campfire workshop; seeds, pets, and more.

The Campfire Keeper has appeared. Taken notice of your contributions to the heat of the campfire, the Campfire Keeper proposes a ritual to make your pet more powerful. Gather required items from existing campfire friends and bring a pet as well. What sort of new powerful pet mutation awaits?
