Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Grow a Garden Guides is a community guide site for Grow a Garden event information. This page explains what information may be collected when you use the site.

Information We Collect

We do not ask visitors to create accounts, submit passwords, or provide personal information directly on this site.

Basic technical information may still be collected automatically by services that host, secure, measure, or monetize the site. This can include browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, referring pages, and similar usage information.

Advertising and Cookies

This site may use Google AdSense or similar advertising services. Advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and personalize ads where allowed.

Google and its advertising partners may use information from your visits to this and other websites to show ads that are more relevant to you. You can learn more about how Google uses advertising cookies at Google Advertising Technologies.

You can manage Google ad personalization at My Ad Center. You can also control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

The site links to third-party platforms such as Discord, Twitter, Roblox, GitHub, and Google services. Those websites have their own privacy policies and settings.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended as a general game guide. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information was shared with us by mistake, please use the Contact page.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as the site changes, especially if new advertising, analytics, or community features are added.
