Campfire Keeper

The Campfire Keeper is here. Speak to him to learn how to perform an inferno ritual to power up your pet with a new mutation. The ritual requires a desiginated pet and inferno shards earnt from submitting fruits to Bearnaby.

Activate the ritual by reaching tier 1 in the campfire. The higher the campfire tier, the faster the ritual is performed. Once the ritual is complete your pet will recieve the Inferno mutation.

Note that the ritual consumes the inferno shards you place in, and that any existing pet mutations on the selected pet will be replaced with inferno once complete.
