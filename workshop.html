Campfire Workshop

Once you have your campfire roaring, head over to the campfire workshop to get your DIY crafting going.

Getting your campfire burning hotter unlocks higher crafting tiers. Each tier unlocking new crafting recipes. Keep in mind, these recipes will only be craftable and the timers will only continue going, when the appropriate campfire tier is burning. So keep that campfire fueled and burning and your crafts well and earning.

Recipes can be found under the respective plant > pet > gear > cosmetic pages.
